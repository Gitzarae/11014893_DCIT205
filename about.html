About

Welcome

Since its founding, the Department of Computer Science at University of Ghana has been dedicated to advancing the field through innovative education and research. As one of the pioneering CS programs in the region, our founders established a vision for harnessing emerging technologies to solve real-world problems.

Since We now offer comprehensive degree options from bachelor's to PhD, immersing students in both technical skills and cross-disciplinary applications. Nationally-ranked specialized master's tracks in fields like data science, cybersecurity and AI prepare our graduates to hit the ground running in diverse career paths. Hands-on learning through projects, startups and external internships gives them an edge over peers elsewhere.

Our stellar faculty conduct pioneering research that makes scholarly publications and makes real impacts. From robotics and computer vision to healthcare analytics and crypto, collaborations push new frontiers. Students actively join multi-year studies that could lead to breakthrough solutions.
Beyond academics, our supportive culture aims to inspire every mind. We advocate solutions to diversify participation in STEM through on- and off-campus partnerships. Students say this sense of belonging prepares them to build workplaces where all people thrive alongside emerging technologies.

As technologies continue evolving at exponential rates, integrating ever deeper into our daily lives and industries, our department's dedication to developing the next generation of responsible leaders remains steadfast. With state-of-the-art facilities, an esteemed faculty, and robust ties to partners advancing innovations that enhance humanity, we are uniquely positioned to empower problem-solvers ready to drive progress through responsible computing.

Excellence in Teaching

Our department is dedicated to providing an excellent, transformative educational experience for students. Through active learning approaches, personalized attention, and flexible, industry-relevant curriculum, our acclaimed faculty nurture both technical skills and problem-solving abilities. Their passion and modern teachings ensure graduates emerge prepared to make a positive influence through responsible computing.

Accessiblity for All

Through flexible online courses, transfer pathways, credit for prior learning, and advising support, we aim to increase access to computer science for all students regardless of background. Early exposure programs and open resources help broaden participation, while summer bridge offerings and peer mentors ease transitions to college. Our goal is developing talent from every group through accessible exploration of computing.

Partnering for Impact

We pursue strong partnerships across industry, education, and communities to foster collaboration on joint research, student apprenticeships, dual enrollment programs, and workforce development. International ties and open educational resources also broaden our impacts. Through cooperation across sectors, our department maximizes benefits for students, organizations, and society.

The Computer Science
Department Vision

So, what distinguishes our students, faculty, and members of the community?
Above all, it is our unwavering optimism and belief in the unlimited possibilities. It is a link to people both near and far.
A desire for socioeconomic transformation that drives us to confront obstacles and make progress. It is the belief that by working together, we can make the world a better place.
Ultimately, our vision is to excel as a world-class public research institution while using technology to benefit humanity. By cultivating responsible innovation, empowering learners and sharing knowledge openly, we will help build a more just and prosperous future for all.
