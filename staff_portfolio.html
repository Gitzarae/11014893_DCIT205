Accomplished Academics

Welcome to the Computer Science Department at the University of Ghana! Our department is comprised of a talented and dedicated staff who are passionate about computer science education and research. With their expertise in diverse areas of computer science, our staff members are committed to providing a stimulating learning environment and advancing knowledge in this rapidly evolving field. Whether it's through innovative teaching methodologies or cutting-edge research projects, our staff members are at the forefront of shaping the next generation of computer scientists. We invite you to explore our department and engage with our exceptional staff as you embark on an exciting journey in computer science.

Mark Atta Mensah

He is a computer science educator and IT leader with over a decade of experience. Certified in Data Science and AI by IBM, He excels in tools like Jira, GitHub, and Tableau. His track record includes successfully managing projects and guiding students to internships at Fortune 500 companies. Adept at agile project management, he brings a potent mix of academic expertise and practical leadership to any tech initiative.

Michael Agbo Tettey Soli

He is a solutions engineer and software architect with over a decade in software engineering. He thrives on solving complex problems using a strong mathematical foundation, particularly interested in Graph Theory, Algorithms, Machine Learning, and SDLC. Proficient in Java, NoSQL, JavaScript, and experienced in domains like Cyber Security and Financial Markets. His expertise extends to building Recommender Systems and working with Neo4j.

Isaac Wiafe

He is dedicated to advancing persuasive technology design. Recognizing a gap in theoretical foundations for current technologies, he focuses on developing tools for analyzing persuasion contexts within the Persuasive Systems Design model. His 3-Dimensional Relationship between Attitude and Behaviour model serves as an effective guide for this analysis. He explores road maps for selecting features and methods that ensure adaptability to users' needs.

Ferdinand Apietu Katsriku

He holds a distinguished academic background, including a PhD from London, PGCE, QTS, and MSc from Scotland, and an MEng with Distinction from the former USSR. He holds a Diploma in Education in Russian Language from the former USSR. His primary research focus is on wireless communications, anticipating their crucial role in future communication systems, particularly in developing countries where the cost of deploying wired lines is challenging.

Matilda Wilson

She is a Lecturer in Computer Science at the University of Ghana, Legon. With a background as a System Analyst at the West African Examinations Council, she has served as the Refinery Programmer and IT Administrator on the IBM AS-400 at the Refinery. Matilda has played key roles as the Refinery IT trainer and Computer Security Administrator, showcasing expertise in system analysis and IT administration.

Edward Danso Ansong

He is an Application Security and Forensic Analyst, Data Centre Engineer, and Cyber Security Expert with certifications from IBM, RedHat, and Oracle. Awarded for research contributions in India and Ghana, he is the Principal Investigator of Fredan Computers Inc, specializing in security consultancy and training. Currently a lecturer and researcher at the University of Ghana, Legon, His expertise lies in Computer Security, Operating Systems, and Networking.

The Computer Science Department
Vision Of The Staff For Students

We envision a Computer Science Department where students thrive as adept, ethical tech leaders. Our commitment is to deliver a forward-looking curriculum, blending core knowledge with emerging technologies. Through collaborative learning and real-world projects, we aim to instill problem-solving skills and a love for continual learning. Our focus extends beyond technical proficiency to nurture well-rounded individuals, emphasizing communication, teamwork, and leadership. We are dedicated to fostering an inclusive culture where every student feels valued. As faculty, we aim to be mentors, guiding you through an educational journey that fosters community and curiosity. Together, we aspire to shape the future of technology and inspire the next generation of innovators. Welcome to a transformative experience in the world of computer science.
